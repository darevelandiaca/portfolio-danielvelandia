Freelance Book Design, Layout and Typography | Bogotá, COL & Paris, FRA

Artist Catalogue - Marcos Ávila Forero

Desde las montañas (From the Mountains) and Contra la Corriente (Against the Currents) are two complementary Artist books that compile the prolific works of Marcos Ávila Forero —a Franco-Colombian visual, performance and conceptual artist based in Paris, France— that came together as a companion piece to his show on the Pori Art Museum in Finland.

The book was designed to be read from end to mid-point and then flipped over, to give both sections the same importance and to defy the notions of conventinal reading order.
